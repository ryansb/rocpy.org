Rochester Python User Group

RocPy is Rochester's finest (and only) Python user group. We strive to get work done in Python. We're here to give others the opportunity to meet and work with other Python developers/enthusiasts.

We meet monthly, and our schedule can be found on Meetup. Presentations typically last 45-60 minutes and are followed by some discussion.

We eventually plan to also hold lightning talks before or after meetings so people can give talks in less depth.

Joining

Joining is simple, just follow the "Meetup" link at the bottom of this page and RSVP to our next meetup.

New faces are always welcome, both in the audience and on the podium. If you have an idea for a presentation show up at a meeting and let us know you'd like to present. We'll find a free presentation slot and you can start preparing your slides.
