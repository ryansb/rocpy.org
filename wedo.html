The Meetings

Meetings are on the third Tuesday of every month, and typically are a 45-minute presentation by a member followed by discussion and demos.

Following a meeting, we typically have a workshop the following Saturday on the same topic.

The Workshops

In the past we've had workshops on applications like Django where participants help each other build small, completely functional projects in an afternoon.

Workshops are hands-on followups to topics discussed at the monthly meetings. They typically start with a more in-depth demo than there was time for at the meeting.
